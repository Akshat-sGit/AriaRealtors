Relish your retreat in a gated township

Sprawling across acres of enchanting development, Eldeco City is designed to relax your mind, refresh your soul. And make you and your family feel safe and secure, in a gated complex with boundary wall and manned gates.

Own an exclusive villa or build a home of your choice on a plot

Eldeco City offers exclusive villas and plots from 86.12 Sq. Mtr. to 214 Sq. Mtr. Endowed with modern facilities and amenities, these villas offer a living experience hitherto unknown. Each villa is expandable, which means you can buy a smaller module as per your requirement today, and then construct additional spaces and rooms to it as per your family’s growing needs (and budget)! Plots are also available for those looking to manifest their design thoughts while drawing the blueprint of their cherished abode.

Rejuvenate your spirits at your own club

At Eldeco City, everything has been designed for a lifestyle that you rightfully deserve. An exclusive club allows you and your family to indulge in high-powered sporting action; or chill in the refreshing waters of the swimming pool, or simply lounge around at the sprawling club lawns.
